Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process take? Most reviews finish within 7-14 days depending on adviser availability.
What file formats are accepted? Upload PDF as the final copy. Drafts can include DOCX and attachments.
Can I extend my submission deadline? Yes. Submit an extension request to your adviser for approval.
How do I update metadata after approval? Open a support ticket and include the corrected details.
